Our Mission

Pharaoh's Path addresses the systemic lack of fair, efficient, and technology-driven mechanisms for talent discovery in Egypt's sports ecosystem.

We're building a centralized, data-driven platform that facilitates transparent communication between football players and scouts, supports video-based performance evaluations, and enables fair scouting regardless of location or socioeconomic background.

Fair Opportunity
Transparent Process
Data-Driven Decisions

The Problem

Traditional scouting in Egypt faces significant challenges that limit opportunities for talented players.

Visibility Challenges

Players without connections struggle to be seen by scouts, regardless of their talent and potential.

Inefficient Process

Scouts face overwhelming workloads, making it difficult to identify and evaluate talent effectively.

Fraud & Bias

The recruitment process is often marred by bribery, favoritism, and lack of transparency.

Our Solution

Pharaoh's Path provides a comprehensive platform with features designed for players, scouts, and administrators.

For Players

  • Create comprehensive profiles with photos and career history
  • Upload performance videos to showcase skills
  • Receive notifications for tryouts and scout feedback
  • Participate in local tournaments organized through the platform

For Scouts

  • Use advanced filters to search players by age, skill, and region
  • View player profiles with performance data and videos
  • Send tryout invitations and track responses
  • Access analytics and reports on scout-player interactions
